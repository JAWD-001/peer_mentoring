Welcome to ManUp

We're a online peer mentorship platform exclusively for men. Approximately 1 in 3 men report having no other man that they feel comfortable enough to chat about the most pressing and stressful parts of their life. We're here to change that. If you're seeking mentorship to overcome some of the challenges that you're currently facing, then we're the platform for you.

Join Groups - Create your community

Join groups with like-minded people facing similar difficulties in a space that's as anonymous and as safe as you make it. Grow your personal community through joining a group, or many!

Meet Mentors or Become One - Get real advice

Need someone to give you pointed, specific, and helpful advice? Open up a new message post in a group and you can get real-time advice from members of your community!

Have Real-Time Chats - Talk it out, right now

Need help facing a problem that can't wait? Jump in the group chat and talk it out with people that are on right now too. Get the best advice for pressing matters from people that have faced them too.
